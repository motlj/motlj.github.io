Now, this is a story all about how my Tacos got flipped-turned upside down, and I'd like to take a minute just sit right there, I'll tell you how I became the prince of a site about tacos.


It was a blustery winter evening late in December 2015. Alaska in the winter time can be a beautiful place. However, this was not the case for the founder of Tacos Tacos Tacos. Enter Cornelious Oswald. Cornelious had just finished a long day at the office, getting told by 7 bosses to do the same job 7 different ways and at 7 different speeds. He had had just about all he could take. When Cornelious got home from work that night he opened his fridge to look for dinner ideas. "Burgers?" Cornelious asked himself. "No, how about Chicken Alfredo?" his subconscious retorted. Cornelious went back and forth with himself for some time. Nothing quite sounded good enough. Until it hit him. "TACOS!" he shouted. The rest as they say is history

Well, that history does indeed have a story. If you'd like to know it, read on. Cornelious ended up making way to many Tacos that night. In an effort to get rid of them, he shared them with all of his neighbors. Lucky for Cornelious, only 3 people that he shared with got food poisoning. The other person loved them. That was all the reinforcement he needed. The next day he walked right into his office and placed a plated taco on each of his 7 bosses desks. On the plate he wrote a message in taco sauce that read "I quit, and I don't want to taco 'bout it."

From that point on, it was Tacos all the time. Cornelious has dedicated every waking minute trying to make the perfect taco. Different meats, seasonings, toppings, sauces. You name it, Cornelious has tried it. Sometimes they haven't turned out the greatest. Take the Chocolate Pudding and meatloaf taco in a spinach tortilla. YUCK! But he's never given up. Even though his business has driven him into crippling debt and took his house, he won't quit. When his wife left him, do you think he quit? Absolutely not. He made a taco out of his very own tears and delivered it to her. She said it was salty and threw it at him. Heart-broken and lonely, Cornelious really focused on his craft. But every taco he made seemed to get worse and worse. With no money, no house and no family, Cornelious was ready to throw in the towel and become a pirate, sailing the world in a quest for buried treasure.

Then one day, it clicked. And Cornelious created Tacos Tacos Tacos Most Boring Taco in the World ®. He had finally created a taco that was edible enough to eat without projectile vomiting. Success has never tasted so mediocre. His wife caught wind of the new taco and came running back to him. The bank forgave all of his loans and even gave him his house back. This was great news, as Cornelious was not cut out to be a pirate. Cornelious suffers from extreme seasickness. He also is deathly afraid of water and of being killed by a sword. Instead, Cornelious went on to create numerous amazing tacos. World renowned even. Some critics have said that there isn't a rating high enough for Tacos Tacos Tacos. Turns out all it took was one incredibly boring taco to make Cornelious' dreams come true.

Becoming a Pirate, Cornelious? I don't think so. Better stick to something you're good at, like Tacos!

Meet Cornelious:

Cornelious Oswald was born on March 9th, 1978 on the small island of Adak, Alaska. Cornelious was the youngest of 14 children. Often forgot about at dinnertime, Cornelious had to come up with a way to feed himself. This is where his love of tacos originated. Today, Cornelious owns the island of Adak, Alaska and has given jobs at Tacos Tacos Tacos to all 326 of its residents. He is locally referred to as King Taco.
