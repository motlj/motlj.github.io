Hi! I'm Josh

As you've seen, my name is Josh Motl. I was born on December 22nd, 1988 in the beautiful state of Wisconsin. I was raised in West Bend & moved to Milwaukee as an adult. I currently work as a quality control analyst for a calibration company here in Milwaukee. My dream is to work as a developer for a fast-paced & fast-growing business.

During my free time, I try to enjoy the outdoors as much as possible. There's not much I enjoy more than going up north for a relaxing weekend at my family's cabin. While there, I fish, hike, ride the ATV trails, & generally just try to soak up as much of mother nature's goodness as I can before heading back home & back to the daily grind.

I am also an avid Wisconsin sports fan. I will cheer for the Packers, Brewers, Bucks, & Badgers win or lose, rain or shine. What's better than a piping hot beer-brat & an ice cold beer during a Sunday tailgate in Wisconsin? You're right, not a whole lot.
